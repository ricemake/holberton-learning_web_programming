Lowell High School is located on Eucalyptus Drive in San Francisco, it has more than 2,600 students
and more than 50 clubs. The colors of the high school are white and cardinal, our mascot is also a
cardinal. Lowell teaches languages from French and Hebrew to Italian and Latin, Lowell also has sports
like spirit squad and badminton. We have 2-3 counselors for each grade and we have more than 50 teachers.
Lowell gives free tutoring in the library for students who are falling behind and we also have clubs likes
the math club to help students who are falling behind in math.
